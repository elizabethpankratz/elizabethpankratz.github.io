Time to stitch!

As an aside: at this point, I can most clearly illustrate why the hand-welted construction is so good for resoling. Should these boots ever need resoled, it‚Äôs this thread that would be cut through to detach the midsole from the uppers. OK, back onto the rails! The last step before moving on to the outsole is to finish the exposed leather of the welt and the midsole.
