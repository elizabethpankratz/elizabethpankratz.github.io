Hi, I'm Elizabeth (she/her), and I'm a PhD student at the University of Edinburgh's Centre for Language Evolution, supervised by Jennifer Culbertson and Simon Kirby. My research looks at the evolution of linguistic structure, with a focus on morphology.

Some other things I care about:

  • morphological productivity and how to measure it
  • the interplay of language usage and cognition
  • pragmatic reasoning, especially scalar inferencing
  • statistical methods
  • NLP for morphologically complex languages

Previously, I did an MSc in Cognitive Systems: Language, Learning, and Reasoning at the Universität Potsdam under the supervision of Shravan Vasishth and Titus von der Malsburg, and before that, an MA in Linguistics at the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin under the supervision of Anke Lüdeling.
